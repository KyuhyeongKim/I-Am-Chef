About Company Page


The site connects travelers and local hosts for meals. The local host posts the desired date and number of people. The traveler applies to the host he likes and eats together. To help your choose, the site offers a variety of reviews and information. You can leave a review after using it and contact us anytime if you have any inconvenience.


This business is a meal sharing platform. When people travel, they want to experience the intact culture of their destination. However, it is difficult to experience meals that are not eaten at famous restaurants, but are usually enjoyed by local families. We offer opportunities to connect local families with travelers. While eating together, you can experience local home food, cultural exchange, and become a chef. I hope our service will contribute to the new travel culture and global cultural exchange.
